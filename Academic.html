Primary School Program

Building strong foundations for lifelong learning

Grades 1-5 (Ages 6-10)

Our Primary School program focuses on developing fundamental skills in reading, writing, and mathematics while nurturing curiosity and creativity. We emphasize:

  • Literacy and numeracy foundations
  • Hands-on science exploration
  • Social studies and cultural awareness
  • Arts and physical education
  • Social-emotional learning
Curriculum Details Sample Schedule

Middle School Program

Transitioning to greater independence and academic rigor

Grades 6-8 (Ages 11-13)

Our Middle School program bridges elementary and high school with a challenging curriculum that encourages exploration and personal growth. Key features include:

  • Subject-specific teachers
  • STEM and humanities integration
  • Advisory program for social support
  • Elective courses in arts and technology
  • Leadership opportunities
Course Catalog Extracurriculars

High School Program

Preparing for academic success and future careers

Grades 9-10 (Ages 14-15)

Our High School program offers a comprehensive curriculum that prepares students for senior secondary education and beyond. Highlights include:

  • Advanced mathematics and sciences
  • Literature and composition focus
  • World languages program
  • Career exploration opportunities
  • College preparation workshops
Academic Requirements College Prep Info

Senior Secondary Program

Specialized pathways for university and career readiness

Grades 11-12 (Ages 16-18)

Our Senior Secondary program offers specialized streams with rigorous academic preparation for university entrance. Program components:

  • Science, Commerce, and Humanities streams
  • Advanced Placement courses
  • Research projects and internships
  • University counseling services
  • Leadership and service requirements
